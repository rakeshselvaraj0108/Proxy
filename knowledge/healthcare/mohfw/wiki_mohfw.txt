The
Ministry of Health and Family Welfare
(
MoHFW
) is a
ministry
of the
Government of India
charged with
health policy
in India. It is also responsible for all government programs relating to
family planning in India
.
[
1
]
[
2
]
The Minister of Health and Family Welfare holds
cabinet
rank as a member of the
Council of Ministers
. The current minister is
Jagat Prakash Nadda
, while the current Minister of State for Health are
Anupriya Patel
and
Prataprao Ganpatrao Jadhav
.
Since 1955, the Ministry regularly publishes the
Indian Pharmacopoeia
through the Indian Pharmacopoeia Commission (IPC), an autonomous body for setting standards for drugs, pharmaceuticals and healthcare devices and technologies in India.
