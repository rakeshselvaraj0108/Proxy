CIVIL AVIATION REQUIREMENTS SECTION 3 – AIR TRANSPORT

3.1. Denied Boarding: Airlines must first ask for volunteers. If boarding is denied against passenger will, airline shall pay compensation equal to 400% of booked one-way basic fare plus airline fuel charge, subject to a maximum of INR 20,000.
3.2. Cancellation: If not informed 24 hours prior, compensation: Block time up to 1 hr: INR 5,000. Block time 1 to 2 hrs: INR 7,500. Block time > 2 hrs: INR 10,000.
3.3. Refunds: All statutory taxes (UDF, PSF, ADF) must be refunded for all fare types (including non-refundable).
