# Sample MRI Denial Appeal Knowledge

When an MRI claim is denied as not medically necessary, collect these anchors before drafting an appeal:

1. The denial reason exactly as written in the claim letter.
2. The policy clause describing diagnostic imaging coverage.
3. Physician order, clinical notes, symptoms, prior treatment history, and urgency.
4. Any pre-authorization request, approval, denial, or missing-information timeline.
5. The internal appeal deadline and external review rights.

A strong appeal should ask the insurer to identify the exact exclusion relied upon, request review by a qualified clinical reviewer, attach physician support, and preserve appeal deadlines.
