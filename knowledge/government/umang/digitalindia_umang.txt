UMANG
UMANG is a unified portal aggregating major citizen-centric services of central and state governments, local, autonomous & statutory bodies through mobile apps (Android & iOS) and the web. It is a Digital India initiative intending to improve ease of living by putting power in the hands of citizens to avail major government services anytime, anywhere, with just a few clicks on a single mobile app.
Since its launch on November 23, 2017, UMANG has grown to host 1745 services from about 80 central departments, ministries & autonomous bodies and 30 States; the User base has grown to 5.58 crore increasing daily. Cumulative transactions through UMANG are 383 crores.
The government’s vision is to position UMANG as a single interface for citizens to interact with the government, be it services, beneficiary schemes, documents, information, engagement/ participation in governance and so on.
