About e-Shram Portal
The Ministry of Labour & Employment which is one of the oldest and important Ministries of the
Government of India, is continuously working on improving life and dignity of Labour force of
country by protecting & safeguarding the interest of workers, promotion of welfare and providing
social security to the Labour force both in Organized and Unorganized Sectors by enactment and
implementation of various Labour Laws, which regulate the terms and conditions of service and
employment of workers.
Accordingly, Ministry of Labour & Employment has developed eShram portal for creating a National
Database of Unorganized Workers (NDUW), which will be seeded with Aadhaar. It will have details
of name, occupation, address, occupation type, educational qualification, skill types etc. for
optimum realization of their employability and extend the benefits of the social security
schemes to them. It is the first-ever national database of unorganised workers including migrant
workers, construction workers, gig and platform workers, etc.
