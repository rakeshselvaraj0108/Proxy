ABOUT DIGILOCKER
Document Wallet to Empower
Citizens
DigiLocker is a flagship initiative of Ministry of Electronics & IT
(MeitY) under Digital India programme. DigiLocker aims at 'Digital Empowerment' of citizen by providing
access to authentic digital documents to citizen's digital
document wallet. DigiLocker is a secure cloud based platform for storage, sharing and verification of
documents & certificates.
