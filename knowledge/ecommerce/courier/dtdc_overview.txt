DTDC Express Limited
, trading as
DTDC
(short for
Desk to Desk Courier & Cargo
), is an Indian logistics and delivery services company, headquartered in
Bengaluru
.
[
7
]
History
[
edit
]
In 2006,
Reliance Capital
acquired a 44% stake from co-founder Debasish Chakraborty, who left the company owing to differences with his elder brother. Subhasish Chakraborty retained his 56% stake in the company, while Reliance Capital reduced its shareholding to 40% later that year.
[
8
]
In 2012, DTDC acquired a 52% stake in Eurostar Express of Eurostar Group in the
UAE
.
[
9
]
In April 2013, DTDC acquired 70% of Nikkos Logistics.
[
10
]
In June 2013, French courier company
Geopost
(owned by
La Poste
) acquired
Reliance Capital
's 39.51% stake in DTDC, taking its overall ownership in DTDC to 42%.
[
11
]
In February 2025, DTDC started
quick-commerce
delivery services, opening its first
dark store
in Bangalore.
