Privacy Policy Statement
This website does not automatically capture any specific personal information from you
(like your name, phone number, or e-mail address) that would allow individual identification.
If personal information is requested, you will be informed of the purpose for collecting it,
and security measures will be applied to protect it.
Personal information will not be shared or sold to third parties. Data is safeguarded
against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
Technical information like IP address, browser type, date/time, and visited pages may be
recorded but not linked to individual identities unless required for security or investigation.
This website/platform is maintained by the Department of Consumer Affairs.
